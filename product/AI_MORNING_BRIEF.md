# Vision

The AI Morning Brief is the heart of eY OS.

Rather than presenting dozens of widgets and notifications, it provides a concise executive briefing every morning, helping the user understand what matters most and what action should be taken first.

The goal is to reduce information overload and improve daily decision making.

The Morning Brief should feel like a trusted Chief of Staff summarising everything important in under 30 seconds.

---

# Product Principles

The Morning Brief should:

• Prioritise decisions over data.
• Summarise instead of listing.
• Highlight changes since yesterday.
• Recommend actions.
• Be calm, concise and informative.
• Never overwhelm the user.

---

# Primary Objective

Answer one question:

> **"What do I need to know today?"**

# Intelligence Categories

The AI should evaluate information using the following categories.

## 1. Priorities

What must be completed today?

Examples

• Liverpool property offer
• Supplier payment
• Swimming session

---

## 2. Changes

What changed since yesterday?

Examples

• New email received
• Supplier replied
• Property status updated
• Weather warning

---

## 3. Risks

What requires attention?

Examples

• Payment overdue
• Mortgage expires soon
• Calendar conflict

---

## 4. Opportunities

What positive opportunities exist?

Examples

• Mortgage rates reduced
• New investment property listed
• Cashback offer available

---

## 5. Recommendation

What should the user do first?

Example

Review the Liverpool property offer before lunch.

# Success Criteria

The Morning Brief should allow the user to understand their day within 30 seconds.

After reading it, the user should know:

• What matters today.
• What has changed.
• What needs attention.
• What action should be taken first.

If those four questions are answered clearly, the Morning Brief has achieved its purpose.
